Business Overview

Unique Decor is a startup business in event decoration with 4 employees, 3 laptops, one Wi-Fi network, and digital customer information used for bookings and payments.

4 Employees 3 Laptops Startup Business

Network Overview

Internet ↓ Router / Firewall ↓ Wi-Fi Network ├─ Laptop 1 (Employee) ├─ Laptop 2 (Employee) └─ Laptop 3 (Owner)

Assets, Threats, and Vulnerabilities

Asset Threat Vulnerability Risk Level
Wi-Fi Network Unauthorized access Weak password High
Employee Laptops Malware No antivirus installed High
Customer Data Data breach Poor access control High
Email Accounts Phishing Weak passwords Medium
Business Files Data loss No backups Medium

Top Security Improvements

Wi-Fi
Use a stronger wireless password and enable WPA2 or WPA3 to reduce the chance of unauthorized network access.
Laptops
Install antivirus software, enable updates, and keep devices patched to reduce malware risk.
Backups
Store important business files in secure cloud backups or on encrypted external storage.

Incident Response Plan

1
Disconnect the affected laptop from Wi-Fi immediately.
2
Run a full malware and antivirus scan on the device.
3
Change passwords for email and business accounts.
4
Inform the business owner and monitor for suspicious activity.
